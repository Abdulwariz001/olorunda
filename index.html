OLORUNDA LOCAL GOVERNMENT (OSOGBO)



Olorunda is a Local Government Area in Osun State, Nigeria. Its headquarters are in Igbona, on the outskirts of the state capital Osogbo.
It has an area of 97 km2 and a population of 131,761 at the 2006 census.The postal code of the area is 230.

Osogbo city seats the Headquarters of both Osogbo Local Government Area (situated at Oke Baale Area of the city) and Olorunda Local Government Area (situated at Igbonna Area of the city).
It is some 88 kilometers by road northeast of Ibadan.
It is also 108 kilometres by road south of Ilorin(kwara state) and 108 kilometres northwest of Akure.

Olorunda local government area is stationed in Osun state,South-west geopolitical division of Nigeria.The headquarters of the LGA reside in the town of igbomina with the LGA constituted of serveral towns and villages such as Oba ife,Oba oke,Osolo,Odesola,Amusan,Awoniyi,Jagun and Onigiliro.

The current estimated population of Olorunda LGA is put at 179,444 inhabitants with the area mostly populated by members of the Yoruba ethnic group.The Yoruba language is commonly spoken in the LGA while the religion of Christianity and Islam are widely practiced in the area.

Olorunda LGA covers a total area of 97 square kilometres and has an average temperature of 29 degree centigrade .The average humidity level in the LGA is 58 percent while the estimated average wind speed is 10km per hour.
